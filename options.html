This Chrome extension allows you to filter out commenters by name in the replies to articles posted on WillBrownsberger.com.

To use it, list the names of commenters you don't want to see in the text box below, separated by semicolons. If you would also prefer not to see the replies to their comments, check the checkbox as well. Then save your preferences and reload any pages on the site that you're already viewing, and you should see your settings take effect.
